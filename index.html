    Smort Code

    Dynamic Liquidity Pool:

    Our token features a unique liquidity pool that is comprised of two different types of tokens.

    Static Liquidity Tokens:

    Representing 30% of the Initial LP Tokens and 10% of LP Tokens from Swapbacks. These tokens can't be removed by the Buyback function and will always increase in number.

    Fluid Liquidity Tokens:

    Representing 70% of the Initial LP Tokens and 90% of LP Tokens from Swapbacks. These tokens are subject to the Buyback function, meaning they may increase or decrease in number.

    Revolutionary Buyback Mechanism:

    With the Buyback function, $PEPOODLE Token will remove LP Tokens and purchase $PEPOODLE with the ETH received. The tokens received will then be burned, reducing the overall supply and increasing scarcity. The Buyback function will run every 3 hours, removing 5% of Fluid Liquidity each time. On the first day, roughly 35% will go towards the Buyback, with a further 10% of LP Tokens being removed 1 minute after the tax reduction.

    Swapback Function:

    As taxes are collected, they will be sold for ETH and added to the LP pool. This allows for constant growth of the liquidity pool, as well as a reduction in the overall supply of $PEPOODLE tokens.

    Dynamic Tax Structure:

    Our token features a normal tax structure of 4%/9%, but with the added benefit of the 'Sacrifice' mechanic. By sacrificing 3% of your balance tokens to PapaPepoodle and PepoodleJR, you will receive reduced taxes (3%/4%) for the number of tokens equal to your balance at the time of sacrifice. To be eligible for this reduced tax rate, simply send 1 token from your address to your address. Those who have sacrificed are also eligible for random airdrops through the GibTreat mechanism.

    The PapaPepoodle and PepoodleJR Mechanics:

    In our system, the PapaPepoodle is the biggest buyer of $PEPOODLE, sorted by ETH amount from all buys. The PapaPepoodle receives 20% of all buy and sell transfer taxes, as well as 40% of all tokens from sacrifices made by others. To become the PapaPepoodle, you must have made a sacrifice and held onto your tokens. On the other hand, to become the PepoodleJR, simply buy more than 0.3 ETH worth of $PEPOODLE. As the PepoodleJR, you will receive 40% of all sell taxes, 40% of the tax from all buys less than 0.3ETH, 40% from the next PepoodleJR buy, and 60% of all tokens from sacrifices made by others.

    GibTreat:

    At random times, the marketing wallet will trigger the GibTreat airdrop. Three lucky winners will receive an airdrop of 50% of the tokens in the token contract divided by 3, at the time of the drop OR a single winner can receive 50% of the tokens in the token contract. To be eligible, simply make the sacrifice and become a part of the $PEPOODLE Token community.
